‚öôÔ∏è MATCH PREPARATION ‚Äì RESTRICTIONS

üöÄ ADVANCED CATEGORY ROBOT ATHLETES

  1. Referee places cross in center of arena dividing it into 4 equal parts
  2. Robot Athletes placed in 2 sections facing each other
  3. Each Robot Athlete must cover white line/ring at least partially
  4. Referee signals to place Robot Athletes. Technicians place simultaneously
  5. Referee removes cross when robots placed. Once placed, cannot be moved
  6. Referee connects Robot Athletes to controller for simultaneous start
  7. At referee signal, technicians move ‚â•1 step back from arena
  8. Referee counts 3,2,1 and activates Robot Athletes

Advanced Setup: Cross divider with blade deployment

üî∞ BASIC CATEGORY ROBOT ATHLETES

  1. Referee places black frame (e.g., cardboard) between robots in center
  2. Black frame divides arena into 4 equal parts. Robots placed in 2 sections
  3. Technicians place robot anywhere in their section. Must cover white line/ring at least partially
  4. Robots must see black frame and not move until removed
  5. Referee signals technicians to press Start button (robots go into standby)
  6. After button pressed, technicians move ‚â•1 step back
  7. Referee counts 3,2,1, let's go and raises black frame
  8. Robots start moving immediately when referee lifts frame
  9. If robot doesn't start within 5 seconds = lost match, victory to opponent

Basic Setup: Black frame divider (top view)
