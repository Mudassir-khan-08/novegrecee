üéØ GOAL

The aim of robot athletes is to compete in a grappling tournament, with the final result determining the overall champion.

Two robots compete in a melee match.

Robots are given three full minutes for a round, in which either:

  • One of the robots is knocked out, OR
  • Match is timed and 3 judges decide the winner
