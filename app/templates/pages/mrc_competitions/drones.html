üéØ GOAL

The goal of Athletes is to perform a demonstration drone operation lasting 3 minutes, as well as perform a precision mission to be announced on the day of the Race.

The sport requires precision in movements, basic programming knowledge and correct calculations.

üë• TEAM ‚Äì COACH

  • Participation: Team-based, not individual.
  • Team Size: 2-3 members per team.
  • Drone Technician: Each team designates up to 1 Drone Technician.
  • Age Requirement: All team members must be at least 10 years old.
  • Coach: Must be at least 20 years old.
  • Assistant Requirement: 1 assistant for every 3 teams registered.
  • Drone Limit: Only one drone per team; no changing during competition.
  • Emergency Replacement: Only microcontroller/electronics with Head Judge permission.

üèÜ COMPETITION

üß™ DEMONSTRATION

Each team has 3 minutes to complete the missions in the specially designed area.

üìã COMPETITION PROCEDURE

  • Route Definition: Defined exclusively by field markings.
  • Numbering: Take-off/landing pads and hoops carry ascending numbers (1, 2, 3, ‚Ä¶).
  • Path Arrows: Arrows indicate exact path the drone must follow.
  • Mandatory Order: Drone executes elements in numerical order according to arrows.
  • Out of Order: Out of order / against arrows = 0 points for that element.

üß™ TESTING

  • Permitted testing announced by Organizing Committee in official schedule.
  • Outside announced program, testing is not permitted.

üì∂ QUEUE & ACCESS

  • Start order is first-come, first-served in the Technicians' queue.
  • Only the Drone Technician allowed to wait in queue.
  • No other team member may hold a place in queue.
  • Non-compliance may result in loss of place.

üö¶ ATTEMPT START

  • Drone placed at take-off position.
  • Referee blows start whistle; operator must launch within 5 seconds.
  • If 5 seconds elapse, flight is invalid. One restart allowed on first attempt only.

üéØ EXECUTION & SCORING

  • Drone executes precisely following numbering & arrows.
  • Points printed on each pad/hoop awarded only if action is correct and in order.
  • Drone must land accurately on landing spot.
  • Maximum mission duration: 3 minutes. If exceeded, team earns no points.

üîÅ ATTEMPTS & CHECKS

  • Each team has 2 attempts.
  • Round 1 completed for all teams before Round 2 begins.
  • Technical inspection of drones by judges between the 2 rounds.

  • 4. Other cases indicated in the Sport rules.

Team results are not taken into account and are not included in competition results.
