About Robofest

Robofest India is NovatechRobo's autonomous robotics program for students in 2nd grade through college. Student teams design, construct, and program their robots to act independently and compete for trophies in a variety of competitions.

Robofest's mission is to generate excitement among young people for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), develop creativity and problem solving skills, and prepare them to excel in higher education and technological careers.

Scholarship Opportunities

üéì Robofest Participant LTU Scholarship

High School Seniors who have participated in Robofest at any time in any division, and who are planning to attend LTU for any degree program can apply for a $3,000 annual renewable scholarship (for a total of $12,000 over 4 years).

Requirements:

  • Submit an application and a letter of recommendation from one of your Robofest adult coaches or mentors
  • 400-word essay describing your Robofest experience and your career goals
  • Student must meet LTU admission and scholarship criteria
  • Deadline: April 1st

Robofest can assist with the letter if requested. For more information and to submit an application, see "Other LTU Scholarships" at ltu.edu/financial-aid/scholarships-freshmen
